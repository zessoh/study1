Before Trilogy

The Before Trilogy consists of three American films created by Richard Linklater. Beginning with Before Sunrise (1995), and continuing with two sequels, Before Sunset (2004) and Before Midnight (2013), the films were directed by Linklater. The first film was co-written by Linklater and Kim Krizan, and its sequels were co-written by Linklater, Ethan Hawke, and Julie Delpy, who also star in the trilogy.
